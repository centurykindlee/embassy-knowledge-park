# Embassy Knowledge Park -- Premium Township in North Bangalore

## Embassy Knowledge Park

**Embassy Knowledge Park** is an upcoming premium integrated township by
Embassy Group located near Stonehill International School in North
Bangalore. Spread across more than **200 acres**, the development
combines luxury apartments, independent villas, commercial spaces,
retail, and hospitality.
